SquareTagAV Class Reference

Overview

Manage graphics and sound for the "SquareTag" task.

SquareTagAV is the audio-visual "front end" of the SquareTag task. It manages look and feel and graphical and sound resources.

SquareTagAV should transform the unitless geometry of SquareTagLogic into viewable graphics and possibly fun sounds. It "knows about" a SquareTagLogic, so it can base the graphics and sound on data in the SquareTagLogic object. It shouldn't modify the SquareTagLogic object.

SquareTagAV doesn't know when do do graphics and sound behaviors. It's up to some other function or class to coordinate the behaviors of a SquareTagAV object, a SquareTagLogic object, and user input.
