DotrisAV Class Reference

Overview

Manage graphics and sound for the "Dotris" game.

DotrisAV is the audio-visual "front end" of the Dotris game. It manages look and feel and graphical and sound resources.

DotrisAV should transform the unitless geometry of DotrisLogic into viewable graphics and possibly fun sounds. It "knows about" a DotrisLogic object, from which it can do the graphics and sound. It shouldn't modify the DotrisLogic object.

DotrisAV doesn't know when do do graphics and sound behaviors. It's up to some other function or class to coordinate the behaviors of a DotrisAV object, a DotrisLogic object, and user input.
